Optimal Interpolation Points - Chebyshev Points¶

The Chebyshev points for the interval $[-1,1]$ are

$$ x_k = -\cos(\theta_k),\quad k=0,1,\dots,n, $$

where

$$ \theta_k = \frac{k \pi}{n}, \quad k=0,1,\dots,n. $$
